About Truckers Welcome

Truckers Welcome at 1500 Locations and growing!

Truckerswelcome is an open source project developed by volunteers in the tech community to help our front line workers who are facing significant challenges while on the road keeping goods and supplies flowing to our grocery stores, hospitals and communities. Truckers Welcome helps drivers find services that are still available near them during this crisis, using the location on their smartphone. This project is made possible by our generous sponsors and volunteers.

Sponsors

This project is hosted in AWS, relies on the Google Maps API and uses DNS and Email services provided by EasyDNS. Some of these costs are currently covered by our volunteers, we appreciate any corporate sponsors willing to help with resources to keep this project alive during the pandemic.

  • We are grateful to have a major part of our supply chain like Amazon supporting Truckers Welcome by providing the resources need to host Truckers Welcome in the AWS cloud.
  • Standard plan free of charge for 90 days to allow our team to collaborate on this application.
  • Thank you to Google Cloud for free trial credits to access the Google Map APIs.
